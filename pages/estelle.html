Estelle

Estelle, second twin of Evendur and his second wife Shandri, is less secure than her brother. Afraid of what the three “trueblood” siblings thought of her, she quickly fell prey to Evandur’s intimidation. She seeks validation among the “trueblood” siblings and plays as sort of a wildcard since her loyalty is constantly challenged by her twin on the opposing side along with her friendships with both members of the neutral bloc.
