Darvin

Second youngest of house Greycastle and fourth child of Evendur and his second wife Shandri, Darvin is a bit too young to be completely “in” the battle for succession but he still has nominally sides with Isobell. While he did not follow the example of his older brother Evendal and joined the neutral bloc he still holds himself as generally separate from the crisis, rather focusing on honing his own talents
